## Intentional Society Announces $1.8 Million, 10-Year Funding Commitment

**March 15, 2026** — Intentional Society, a community dedicated to relational development and values-aligned action, today announced a $1.8 million funding commitment from a small private family foundation, spread over ten years. The announcement was made at the organization's 5-Year Celebration event, "Launching a New Era," marking Intentional Society's transition from an exploratory, R&D-oriented phase into a mature, integrated ecosystem.

The funding will support three key areas: establishing the IS Workteam, a backbone operational team created in December 2025 to provide sustained capacity for the Intentional Society ecosystem; ongoing contributions to the Intentional Ventures system, which catalyzes values-aligned livelihoods; and grants to partners and allies in the broader liminal space who are doing impactful work in the field. All 2026 allocations have been determined.

"This commitment is a signal of sustainability and perseverance," said James Baker, founder of Intentional Society. "What we've built together over these first five years has deep roots, and this funding grounds our intention to keep growing for the next ten years and beyond. We're not just dreaming — we're building something that lasts."
